Be ready

Logistics & what to bring

📍 The venue

Espira Kinrara, Puchong, Selangor. Accommodation and meals throughout the physical bootcamp are provided. Bring your IC/passport for check-in.

🎒 Packing essentials

Smart-casual wear, one formal outfit for graduation, comfy shoes, laptop and chargers, personal medication. Full packing list goes to confirmed participants.

💻 Virtual setup

For 18–21 July: stable internet, a quiet space, camera on. Zoom links and the session timetable arrive by email and the cohort WhatsApp group before Day 1.

⏰ Sessions run morning to night — rest well 📱 Daily updates land in the cohort WhatsApp group
