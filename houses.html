Leadership within

The House Captain

A few days after the reveal, each House elects one Captain — nominated from within, chosen by the House.

Your Captain is the official bridge between your House and the programme team: they coordinate House submissions, rally the group, and represent you at graduation. Choose someone who shows up for people — not just someone loud.

What Captains do

• Coordinate official House submissions

• Run House polls and discussions

• Keep the group engaged (nudges, not spam)

• Represent the House at graduation
